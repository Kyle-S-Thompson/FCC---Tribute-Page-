About the Doc

Dr. Disrespect is a two time blockbustrer video game world champion whom originated as Beahm playing Halo 2 on the Xbox. With the ability to use proximity voice chat, This created an opportunity for Beahm (Dr.) to not only showcase his dominance, but also rub it in the faces of his adversaries.

Background

On March 16, 2011, the Doc was appointed as the community manager of Sledgehammer Games, and later promoted to level designer and helped create many of the multiplayer maps for Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare. Due to a lack of competition in his professional life, the Doc went on to join Twitch.tv whilst still employed at Sledgehammer. With the rising success of his streaming, he left the company to pursue twitch full time in 2015.
